About Us

Mind Kind is my company. My name is Louis Pavli and I have been researching self development for years. My passion for personal growth and transformation has led me to create an online platform where I can share my knowledge and experience with others.


My journey towards self development began when I was going through a difficult time in my life. I was struggling with addiction and serious negative self talk and I knew that I needed to make a change in my life. That's when I discovered the world of self development.


I started reading books, attending seminars, and learning from experts in the field. I learned that self development is all about improving yourself in all areas of your life, from your physical health to your relationships to your career.


Why Self Development is Important to Us

Self development is so important to us because it allows you to take control of your life. When you invest in yourself and work on improving yourself, you can achieve your goals, overcome challenges, and live a more fulfilling life.


Self development also helps you to become more self-aware. When you take the time to reflect on your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors, you can gain a deeper understanding of yourself and your motivations. This can help you to make better decisions and improve your relationships with others.


In addition, self development can help you to build resilience. Life is full of ups and downs, and when you have the skills and knowledge to cope with challenges, you can bounce back from setbacks and continue to move forward.


Through Mind Blind, I hope to inspire and empower others to embark on their own self development journeys. Whether you're looking to improve your health, boost your career, or cultivate stronger relationships, there is always room for growth and improvement.
